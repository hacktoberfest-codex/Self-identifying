        Seeking Professional Helps

        Seeking professional help for mental health involves consulting trained therapists,
        psychologists or psychiatrists to address emotional, psychological,
        or psychiatric concerns. It can include therapy, medication, or a combination,
        promoting well-being and improved mental health outcomes.

        Community and peer support

        Community and peer support for mental health involves individuals coming
        together to provide emotional assistance, understanding, and encouragement
        to those facing mental health challenges. These groups create safe spaces
        where people can share experiences, coping strategies, and resources, fostering
        a sense of belonging and promoting mental well-being.
