OBENEFITS

Cognitive Health Technology for Health Systems

Early detection and treatment can reduce the expensive clinical and financial burdens of cognitive impairment related issues. Using the BrainCheck platform has helped providers with their ability to increase confidence in their diagnoses, create customized intervention plans, and improve overall quality of cognitive care.

Easy Deployment and Optional EHR Integration​

The BrainCheck platform is suited to easily scale with hospitals and health systems of all sizes. Additionally, our experienced team can work with you to integrate the BrainCheck platform with your EHR.

Supports Fee-for-Service and Value-Based-Care Models at Scale

The BrainCheck platform offers a source of rich, reliable, unbiased data to support stratification and care planning across diverse populations. A pilot quality improvement study utilizing the BrainCheck platform showed an increase of diagnosis specificity, diagnostic workups, and 90% rate of reimbursement assessments administered during the duration of the study.

Efficient In-Clinic and Remote Administration

BrainCheck assessments can be administered by medical assistants or caregivers, in-clinic and remotely, improving efficiency and increasing access while offering cost savings to clinics and hospitals.
