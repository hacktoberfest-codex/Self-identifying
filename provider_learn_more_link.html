OBENEFITS

Advanced Cognitive Health Platform for Providers

Dementia and other forms of cognitive impairment often go undiagnosed. But with recent updates to reimbursement codes and an added requirement for annual wellness exams, providers are now encouraged to assess cognitive function as part of the routine standard of care. BrainCheck’s comprehensive cognitive health platform for providers is specifically designed to help clinicians quickly and easily assess patients’ cognitive function — and get reimbursed.

Provide High-Quality Patient Care

Clinically validated, the BrainCheck Assessment has demonstrated evidence pointing to the ability to increase early detection of cognitive impairment and promote cognitive care management when appropriate.

Add Revenue For Your Practice

Our customers have reimbursed for BrainCheck cognitive assessments using several CPT codes, including CPT 96138 and CPT 96132 for neurocognitive test administration and test interpretation, and CPT 99483 for cognitive care planning.

Improve Operational Efficiency

The BrainCheck platform is easy to use. Typical assessments take around 15 minutes, and tests can be administered by MAs and med-techs, simplifying the entire process and allowing providers to spend more time with patients.
